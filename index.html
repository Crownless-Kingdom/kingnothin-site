Our operating principles

Built on ideas that outlast trends

  • Human first. Technology should serve people, never replace their judgment.
  • Privacy is a right. Not secrecy. The protected space where independent thought can flourish.
  • Agency over convenience. Convenience matters, but never at the cost of autonomy.
  • Discernment before certainty. We encourage thoughtful questioning, intellectual humility, and evidence-based reasoning.
  • Curiosity without fear. The future should be explored with courage, not dread.
  • Open knowledge. Complex ideas become powerful only when ordinary people can understand and use them.
